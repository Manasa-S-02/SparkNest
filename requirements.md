# Requirements Document

## Introduction

The Adaptive Learning Assistant is an AI-powered educational platform that personalizes the learning experience for students. The system generates customized learning materials, adapts question difficulty based on performance, tracks progress using knowledge graphs, and provides targeted reinforcement to address knowledge gaps. The goal is to help students master any topic efficiently through intelligent, adaptive practice and continuous progress tracking.

## Glossary

- **Learning_Assistant**: The AI-powered system that generates lessons, questions, and tracks student progress
- **Student**: A user who interacts with the system to learn topics
- **Topic**: A subject area or concept that a student wants to learn
- **Learning_Material**: AI-generated concise explanations and lessons for a specific topic
- **Question**: An assessment item generated by the system to test student understanding
- **Difficulty_Level**: A classification of question complexity (Easy, Medium, or Hard)
- **Study_Session**: A period during which a student actively engages with learning materials and questions
- **Knowledge_Graph**: A structured representation of topics, subtopics, and their relationships used to track student mastery
- **Mastery_Level**: A measure of how well a student has learned a specific topic or concept
- **Knowledge_Gap**: An area where a student demonstrates insufficient understanding
- **Mini_Lesson**: A short, targeted explanation provided to address specific mistakes or knowledge gaps
- **Progress_Tracker**: The component that records and maintains student performance data across sessions

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Topic-Based Learning Material Generation

**User Story:** As a student, I want to input a topic name and receive concise learning material, so that I can quickly understand new concepts.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student provides a valid topic name, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL generate learning material within 10 seconds
2. WHEN learning material is generated, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL format it in a concise and easy-to-understand structure
3. WHEN a student requests learning material for a topic, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL store the topic in the student's learning history
4. IF a topic name is empty or invalid, THEN THE Learning_Assistant SHALL return an error message and prompt for valid input
5. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL support topic names containing alphanumeric characters, spaces, and common punctuation

### Requirement 2: Adaptive Question Generation

**User Story:** As a student, I want to receive questions at appropriate difficulty levels, so that I can progressively build my understanding.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student begins practicing a topic, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL generate questions starting at Easy difficulty level
2. WHEN generating questions, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL create questions at exactly one of three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, or Hard
3. WHEN a question is generated, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL include the correct answer and explanation
4. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL generate questions that are relevant to the current topic being studied
5. WHEN a student requests a question, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL provide it within 5 seconds

### Requirement 3: Performance-Based Difficulty Progression

**User Story:** As a student, I want the system to adjust question difficulty based on my performance, so that I am appropriately challenged.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student answers all questions correctly at the current difficulty level, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL advance to the next higher difficulty level
2. WHEN a student makes one or more mistakes at the current difficulty level, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL provide a mini lesson before allowing retry
3. WHEN advancing difficulty levels, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL progress from Easy to Medium, or from Medium to Hard
4. IF a student is at Hard difficulty level and answers all questions correctly, THEN THE Learning_Assistant SHALL mark the topic as mastered
5. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL require a minimum of 3 consecutive correct answers before advancing difficulty levels

### Requirement 4: Targeted Remediation

**User Story:** As a student, I want to receive explanations when I make mistakes, so that I can understand my errors and improve.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student answers a question incorrectly, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL identify the specific knowledge gap
2. WHEN a knowledge gap is identified, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL generate a mini lesson addressing that specific concept
3. WHEN a mini lesson is provided, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL present it before allowing the student to retry similar questions
4. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL generate mini lessons that are shorter than full learning materials but comprehensive enough to address the gap
5. WHEN a student completes a mini lesson, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL generate a new question on the same concept to verify understanding

### Requirement 5: Progress Tracking and Session Management

**User Story:** As a student, I want my progress to be saved automatically, so that I can resume learning from where I left off.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a study session begins, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL load the student's previous progress data
2. WHEN a student answers a question, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL immediately persist the answer and result to the database
3. WHEN a student exits and returns, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL restore the exact state including current topic, difficulty level, and question history
4. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL track the timestamp of each study session
5. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL maintain a complete history of all questions attempted and their outcomes

### Requirement 6: Knowledge Graph Construction and Maintenance

**User Story:** As a student, I want the system to understand relationships between concepts, so that my learning path is coherent and builds on previous knowledge.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a new topic is introduced, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL create or update nodes in the knowledge graph
2. WHEN a student demonstrates mastery of a topic, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL update the mastery level for that node in the knowledge graph
3. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL represent relationships between topics and subtopics in the knowledge graph structure
4. WHEN identifying knowledge gaps, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL use the knowledge graph to determine prerequisite concepts
5. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL persist knowledge graph updates immediately after each assessment

### Requirement 7: Mastery Level Assessment

**User Story:** As a student, I want to see my mastery level for each topic, so that I can understand my strengths and weaknesses.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student completes questions, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL calculate a mastery level score between 0 and 100
2. WHEN calculating mastery level, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL consider question difficulty, accuracy, and consistency of correct answers
3. WHEN a mastery level reaches 90 or above, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL mark the topic as mastered
4. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL update mastery levels in real-time as students answer questions
5. WHEN displaying mastery levels, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL show them for each topic and subtopic separately

### Requirement 8: Student Authentication and Data Isolation

**User Story:** As a student, I want my learning data to be private and secure, so that my progress is protected.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student creates an account, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL store credentials securely using industry-standard hashing
2. WHEN a student logs in, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL verify credentials and create an authenticated session
3. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL ensure that each student can only access their own learning data and progress
4. WHEN a student logs out, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL terminate the session and require re-authentication for future access
5. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL enforce session timeouts after 24 hours of inactivity

### Requirement 9: Learning Material Quality and Consistency

**User Story:** As a student, I want learning materials to be accurate and well-structured, so that I can trust the information I'm learning.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN generating learning material, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL structure content with clear sections including introduction, key concepts, and examples
2. WHEN generating content, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL validate that the material is relevant to the requested topic
3. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL generate learning material at an appropriate reading level for the target audience
4. WHEN a topic has been previously generated, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL retrieve cached content to ensure consistency
5. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL limit learning material length to between 200 and 800 words for optimal comprehension

### Requirement 10: Question Variety and Relevance

**User Story:** As a student, I want to receive diverse types of questions, so that my understanding is tested comprehensively.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN generating questions, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL create multiple question formats including multiple choice, true/false, and short answer
2. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL ensure that questions at the same difficulty level test different aspects of the topic
3. WHEN a student has answered a question, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL not repeat the exact same question within the same study session
4. THE Learning_Assistant SHALL generate questions that directly relate to concepts covered in the learning material
5. WHEN generating Hard difficulty questions, THE Learning_Assistant SHALL include questions that require synthesis or application of multiple concepts
